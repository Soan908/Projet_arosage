Existing sprinkler systems > Surface watering 
Description: Surface watering uses hoses and sprinklers placed directly on the ground, allowing for quick and easy installation without any construction work. It is well suited to small gardens, terraces, or vegetable gardens and can be automated with a timer on the tap. However, it is less discreet, can obstruct passage, and sometimes needs to be moved or stored away.
