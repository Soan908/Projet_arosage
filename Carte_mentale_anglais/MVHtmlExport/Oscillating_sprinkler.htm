Existing sprinkler systems > Oscillating sprinkler 
Description: Oscillating sprinklers use a sprinkler head that sweeps water from side to side, like a back-and-forth motion, to cover rectangular or elongated lawns. They are easy to install and effective for medium-sized areas. However, they can cause splashing and water loss in untargeted areas. It is ideal for regular, uniform watering, while remaining economical and easy to move if necessary.
