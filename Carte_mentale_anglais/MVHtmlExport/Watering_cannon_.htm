Existing sprinkler systems > Watering cannon  
Description: Sprinkler irrigation uses a powerful jet of water projected over a long distance, ideal for large lawns, sports fields or fields. It allows large areas to be watered quickly, but consumes a lot of water and can be less accurate, with watering depending on the wind. It is mainly used where speed and range are priorities, rather than water conservation or accuracy.
