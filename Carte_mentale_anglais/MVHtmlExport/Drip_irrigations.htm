Existing sprinkler systems > Drip irrigations 
Description: Drip irrigation is a system that delivers water slowly and directly to the roots through drippers on small pipes, allowing for very precise and economical watering. It is ideal for vegetable gardens, hedges, flower beds and potted plants, and can be easily automated with a timer. It significantly reduces water waste and ensures more consistent and healthy plant growth.
